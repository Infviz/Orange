Bind from view model to control

With the binding


	data-bind="bindings: {property: 'ViewModelProperty', target: 'ControlProperty'}"
	data-bind="bindings: [{property: 'vmProp1', target: 'cProp1'}, {property: 'vmProp2', target: 'cProp2'}]"

it is possible to bind directly from a property (Rx.Observable or knockout observable) on the view, to a getter/setter on a control.

Example using a knockout observable for the x position and a Rx.Observable for the y property.
