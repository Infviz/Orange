Bind from view model to control

With the binding


	data-bind="bindings: {ViewModelProperty:'ControlProperty'}"
	data-bind="bindings: [{vmProp1:'cProp1'}, {vmProp2:'cProp2'}]"
	data-bind="bindings: {ViewModelProperty:'ControlProperty', mode:'twoWay'}"

it is possible to bind directly from a property (Rx.Observable or knockout observable) on the view, to a getter/setter on a control.

If the property on the view model is "settable" (i.e. is a knockout observable or a Rx subject) two way bindings are also possible.
